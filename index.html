Tuberculosis in Migrant Populations Coming to America

2025

What is TB?

Airborne bacterium Mycobacterium tuberculosis infects the lungs, causing latent or active disease.

Pathogenesis

Inhaled bacilli form granulomas; breakdown triggers contagious active TB.

15× Higher TB incidence among migrants vs U.S.-born residents
60% Migrants living in crowded housing—key driver of transmission
1 in 3 People with latent TB who delay care due to stigma
2–3× Treatment completion increase with community‑health‑worker programs
